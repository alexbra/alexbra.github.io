I am a Software Engineer with 10+ years of development experience and I really excited about coding. My main specialization is Front end with some full stack experience. I have experience in development of different software products, from small web sites on WordPress to big enterprise applications.

I always learn something new. In April 2015, graduated from Udacity's Data Analyst Nanodegree to expand my knowledge in Data Science field and understand how data-driven applications works. Especially, I like Data visualization and D3 framework. For the last next months I have been studying Angular 2, create applications on it and contribute to Open Source projects.
