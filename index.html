Hi, I am Alex. I am an software engineer snd data science enthusiast. Working with different reports, SQL queries and data wrangling operations, I have discovered data analysis as a new direction for personal growth. As a first step in my Data Science journey I choose studying at Udacity's Data Analyst Nanodegree. In April 2015, I succesfully graduated from it and finished several projects connected with data. At this portfolio I will publish all my interesting findings and accomplishments.

I continue to develop in this field and am looking for opportunities to explore business and social problems and applicable solutions. My goal is to identify how to improve efficiency and quality based on analytical findings and use Big Data to make effective decisions. It is really exciting to see data's impact on our lives, businesses, environment and society.
