About me

My name is Alex Brazhenko. I have been living in Seattle Area since I moved here from Ukraine in March of 2016. Make a difference in everything I did one of the main satisfaction in my life. Even in code. I like it. Coding is challenging and exciting! Really. You always need to learn something new. You can participate in interesting projects and have fun.

I am a Software Engineer with 10+ years of development experience. My main specialization is Front end. I have experience in development of different software products, from small web sites on WordPress to big enterprise applications. Have background in automation of HR services, talent management, education, e-learning and finance. Familiar with Data analysis.

Since May of 2016 I have been working as a Front-end developer at Microsoft Dynamics CRM dev team (as a vendor).

Previously, in 2009, I took the job at DTEK Energy company as a developer of a corporate learning portal. This is an internal website of the Corporate University. I deployed learning and talent management systems from scratch, which became one of the biggest in Ukraine. During 2012-2016,I was the lead of developers team. Time to time had honor to participate as a speaker at conferences, workshops and webinars in E-Learning every year.

Also I was a web developer in investment company, freelancer, made some websites as a volunteer and even taught Computer Science in a University as a teaching assistant after my graduation in 2003.

I always learn something new. In April 2015, graduated from Udacity's Data Analyst Nanodegree to expand my knowledge in Data Science field and understand how data-driven applications works. Especially, I like Data visualization and D3 framework. For the last next months I have been studying Angular 2, create applications on it and contribute to Open Source projects.

Also have some experience with: Python, RegExpr, R, R Studio, OpenCV, Hadoop, Map Reduce, c#, PHP
